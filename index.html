HOW TO PLAY?

  • Press the space bar to jump over the obstacles in your path.
  • The longer you hold space bar, the higher pokemon will jump.
  • The longer you play, the higher score...
  • ...But the longer you play, the faster pokemon also run!
  • Once you crash into something, the game is over.
  • Beware of coronavirus! Crash with him won't stop game, but
    you'll lost points by infection.

Good luck!
